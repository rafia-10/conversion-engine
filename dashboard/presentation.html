Core Innovation — Act IV Mechanism

The Deterministic Confidence Gate

Every signal is classified in code before the LLM prompt is assembled. The model can only generate text around pre-hedged claims — it cannot assert what the data doesn't support.

HIGH
Assertive: "You closed a $14M Series B six months ago — you're scaling."
MEDIUM
Hedged: "We see signals of a recent funding event at your company."
LOW
Question-framed: "Has there been a recent funding event we should know about?"
ABSENT
Omitted entirely: Signal never appears in the email — no hallucination possible.
